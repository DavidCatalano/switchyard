### Usage Notes
- DEVLOGs are concise orientation — enough to understand where things stand and how we got here.
- Each entry is a dated bullet: what happened, what decisions were made, what's next.
- Target: phase-level summaries, not task-level granularity. Task progress belongs in the PLAN (checked-off items).
- User-initiated: create when the effort benefits from a breadcrumb trail across sessions.
- Keep the total document short. If a DEVLOG is growing beyond ~20 entries, the detail likely belongs in the PLAN or CONTEXT.
